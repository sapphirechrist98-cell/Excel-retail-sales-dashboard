# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard Using Excel

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive retail sales performance dashboard created using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to analyze sales, profit, customer segments, product categories, regional performance, monthly trends, and payment preferences using a simulated retail dataset.

The dashboard enables users to explore performance interactively using slicers for Region, Category, Customer Segment, and Payment Mode.

## Dashboard Preview

![Retail Sales Performance Dashboard](docs/dashboard_screenshot.png)

## Business Objective

The business required a clear view of overall retail performance and wanted to answer the following questions:

- What are the total sales and total profit?
- Which region contributes the highest revenue and profit?
- Which product category performs best?
- Which customer segment contributes the most business value?
- How do sales and profits change month by month?
- Which payment methods are most commonly used?

## Dataset

The project uses a simulated retail sales dataset containing 40 customer orders from January to August 2025.

Key fields include:

- Order ID
- Order Date
- Customer Segment
- Region
- Product Category
- Product
- Sales
- Cost
- Profit
- Payment Mode

## Data Preparation

The following preparation steps were completed before analysis:

- Converted the raw dataset into a structured Excel Table.
- Created a `Month_Start` field for chronological monthly analysis.
- Created a `Profit_Margin` field to compare profitability.
- Formatted numerical fields for clear business reporting.

## Key Insights

1. **Regional Performance:** South generated the highest sales of `1,01,493` and the highest profit of `26,843`. East achieved the strongest approximate profit margin at `30.72%`.

2. **Category Performance:** Furniture recorded the highest sales at `1,14,100`, while Technology produced the highest profit at `31,203`. Office Supplies achieved the highest approximate profit margin at `39.15%`.

3. **Customer Segment Performance:** Corporate customers generated the highest sales of `1,52,194` and profit of `40,009`. Home Office achieved the highest approximate profit margin at `33.02%`.

4. **Monthly Trend:** March recorded the highest sales at `35,344`, while July produced the highest profit at `10,116`.

5. **Payment Mode Analysis:** UPI was the most frequently used payment method with `13` orders, while Credit Card generated the highest sales value at `81,300`.

## Business Recommendations

- Continue prioritizing the South region because of its strong revenue and profit contribution.
- Study East's product and customer mix to understand its stronger profit margin.
- Focus on Technology products for profit growth.
- Explore promotional opportunities for higher-margin Office Supplies products.
- Strengthen relationships with Corporate customers while developing targeted offers for profitable Home Office customers.
- Maintain smooth digital payment options, particularly UPI, Credit Card, and Bank Transfer.
