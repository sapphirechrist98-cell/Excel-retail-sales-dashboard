# Dashboard Plan

## Dashboard Name
Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Top KPI Cards
Create five KPI cards at the top:

1. Total Sales
2. Total Profit
3. Total Orders
4. Average Order Value
5. Profit Margin

## Charts To Create

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend
Chart type: Line chart

Purpose:
Shows how sales changed over time.

### 2. Sales By Region
Chart type: Bar chart

Purpose:
Compares performance across regions.

### 3. Sales By Category
Chart type: Column chart or donut chart

Purpose:
Shows which product category brings the most revenue.

### 4. Profit By Category
Chart type: Bar chart

Purpose:
Shows which category is most profitable.

### 5. Sales By Customer Segment
Chart type: Column chart

Purpose:
Shows which customer segment contributes most revenue.

## Slicers
Add slicers for:

- Region
- Category
- Segment
- Payment Mode

## Recommended Layout
Top row:
KPI cards

Middle row:
Monthly Sales Trend and Sales By Region

Bottom row:
Sales By Category, Profit By Category, and Sales By Segment

## Business Insights Template
Use this format after completing the dashboard:

1. The highest sales came from ______ region.
2. The most profitable category was ______.
3. The strongest customer segment was ______.
4. Sales increased/decreased during ______ month.
5. The business should focus on ______ because ______.
